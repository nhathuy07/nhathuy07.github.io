A message

"What if, instead of relying on pre-made revision quizzes, you can have them generated based on whatever you learn?"
That's what led me to experiment with the idea of AI-generated revision materials for the 30th National Young Competition for Information Processing. This idea, as I see it, would open up to limitless possibility while having the potential to serve as part of a student's digital toolbox. After six months of development and consultation with my mentor - an experienced teacher - Text2Quiz was born as a proof-of-concept demo. Despite having a suboptimal feature set and question generation quality, its potentials for further advancement and commercialization were recognized by the panel of judges - who are lecturers at either Hanoi University of Science and Technology or Vietnam National University.
